Location

284 Nijoaburanokoji-cho, Aburano-koji St. Nijo-sagaru, Nakagyo-ku, Kyoto 604-0051
TEL. +81 75 468 3155 (Reservations)

  • On the subway Tozai Line, alight at Nijojo-mae Station Exit 2 (3min walk)
  • On the subway Karasuma Line, alight at Karasuma Oike Station Exit 2 (10min walk)

By car from Kyoto Station: Approx. 15min
*Please note that the hotel entrance for vehicles and guests is on Aburano-koji Street.

Guests arriving from KIX or ITM airports

  • From Kansai International Airport (KIX) traveling to the Hotel by car: Approx. 100min
  • From Kansai International Airport (KIX) traveling to the Hotel by train (JR Haruka Service to Kyoto Station): Approx. 75min / or by Airport Limousine Bus to Kyoto Station: Approx. 85min
  • From Osaka International Airport (ITM) traveling to the Hotel by car: Approx. 60min
  • From Osaka International Airport (ITM) traveling to the Hotel by Airport Limousine Bus to Kyoto Station: Approx. 50min

Guests arriving by car

  • On the Meishin Expressway take the Kyoto Higashi IC Exit: Approx. 30mins to Hotel
  • On the Meishin Expressway take the Kyoto Minami IC Exit: Approx. 25mins to Hotel

Parking Capacity: 23 vehicles

For Guests traveling from Tokyo

The Shinkansen service from Tokyo Station to Kyoto Station takes 2h 20min
